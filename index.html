LEADERSHIP PHILOSOPHY


✔ I am a strong believer in the role of values in raising responsible citizens. I believe in the impact that early childhood education has on the overall development of a person. As a result, I am passionate about the quality of education and values that young children receive. I believe that every child should receive the personal attention that they deserve and be given the opportunity to grow in confidence and independence. Love every child and support every child.


✔ I believe in leading with transparency and integrity. A leader who cannot live up to the ideals he or she professes is not likely to raise a team that will withstand the test of time.


✔ As a leader, lead with empathy and invest in continuous professional development.


✔ Every leader needs to have a structure that makes it easy for succession because you won't be there forever.
